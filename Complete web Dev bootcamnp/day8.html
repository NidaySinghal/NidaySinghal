Ids and Classes

"id" and "class" are two diffrent attributes that is used to give tag a specific identity, you can give same "class" to many tags but "id" should be strictly diffrent for every tag. There is a shortcut to give a new "div" tag its "id" and "class"... you can type "." then your desired "class" name and type "#" and then your desired "id" and then hitting enter will give your blank "div" container that has your desired "id" and "class".

Giving "id" and "class" to every tag will help you to target multiple or specific tags according to your need and make changes in it in CSS files.
